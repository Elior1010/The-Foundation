Meme Showcase at the University of Advancing Technology

The objective of this project is to explore the cultural impact and communicative power of memes in digital media. This includes:

  • Understanding the role of humor and virality in meme creation and dissemination.
  • Analyzing how memes can be used for effective digital communication and engagement.
  • Developing skills in graphic design and digital content creation through meme-making.
